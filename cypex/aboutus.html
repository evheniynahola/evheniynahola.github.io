Our Journey and Team

Cypex was founded by a group of veteran cyber and intelligence experts, many with backgrounds in Israeli intelligence. Our founders experienced firsthand the limitations of traditional threat intelligence platforms and saw the need for more advanced cybersecurity solutions.

Our Mission
Recognizing the growing complexity of digital threats, our goal was to create a platform that goes beyond surface-level detection.
Our Team
We’re a team of professionals who share a common goal: to innovate and advance the field of cybersecurity. Our team includes seasoned intelligence veterans and top-tier researchers and engineers.
Our Expertise
Cypex is built on a foundation of unparalleled expertise in intelligence, machine learning, data science.
Our Focus
We don’t just identify threats—we uncover the infrastructure, techniques, and methods used by cyber adversaries. This gives our clients a first-mover advantage in threat detection and mitigation.
Continuous Innovation
Our R&D team constantly refines our platform to ensure it remains at the forefront of threat intelligence, delivering the insights organizations need to protect their digital assets and stay ahead of emerging threats.
